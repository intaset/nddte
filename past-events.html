Past Events

The previous event in this conference series, NDDTE, was held on April 4 - 6, 2017, in Barcelona, Spain. NDDTE is one of the 5 conferences that make up the World Congress on Recent Advances in Nanotechnology (RAN). Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the congress becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.

Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Dr. Olaf Walter
    for the paper entitled: "AnO2 Nanocrystals via Hydrothermal Decomposition of Actinide Oxalates"
  • Dr. Thirumany Sritharan
    for the paper entitled: " Investigation of Photocarrier Losses in Pyrite (FeS2) Film Consisting Single Crystal Nanocubes"
  • Dr. Serena Mazzucchelli
    for the paper entitled: "Metronomic Nanocaged Doxorubicin Prevents Chemoresistance and Cardiotoxicity in Breast Cancer"
  • Ignacio Gonzalez Oliva
    for the paper entitled: "A Theoretical-Experimental Comparison of CdSe Quantum Dot Optical Properties"
  • Dr. Abdulilah Dawoud Bani-Yaseen
    for the paper entitled: "Computational Study on the Nanotubes Formation between Olsalazine and β-Cyclodextrin"
  • Dr. Müge Andaç
    for the paper entitled: "Cu(II)-Imprinted Nanoparticles for Real Time Detection of Cu(II) Ions"
