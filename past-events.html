Past Events

The previous event in this conference series, NDDTE, was held on April 10 - 12, 2018, in Budapest, Hungary. NDDTE is one of the 5 conferences that make up the World Congress on Recent Advances in Nanotechnology (RAN). Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the congress becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.

Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Jude Akinyelu, for the paper entitled: " Folic Acid-Conjugated Chitosan Functionalized Gold Nanoparticles for Targeted Delivery of 5-Fluorouracil in Breast Cancer " ;

  • Dr. Samir Belkhiri, for the paper entitled: " Synthesis and Characterisation of Fe-MW Carbon Nanotubes – Nanocrystalline NaY Zeolite " ;

  • Yu-Jin Choi, for the paper entitled: " Fabrication of Actuating and Rewritable Film by Self-Assembled Hierarchical Superstructures " ;

  • Birnur Cömez, for the paper entitled: "Characterization and Stability of Long siRNA Loaded-Chitosan Nanoparticles" ;

  • Lorenzo David, for the paper entitled: " Gold Nanoparticles Conjugated to Encapsulated 5-Fluorouracil Cationic polymers and their Efficiency as Drug Delivery Vehicles " ;

  • Dr. Nokuk Park, for the paper entitled: "Effect of Mn-Based Additive for Enhancing Stability of Cu-Based Oxygen Carriers for CLC Process" ;

  • Nadia Sid, for the paper entitled: " Development and Demonstration of Highly Insulating, Construction Materials from Bio-Derived Aggregates " ;

  • Dr. Sujittra Srisung, for the paper entitled: "Specific and Structural Study of Cu(II), Na(I) and CuNPs on Picolinic Acid Ligand" ;

  • Dr. Marta Truffi, for the paper entitled: " MAdCAM-1 Nanotargeting Uncovers Bowel Inflammation Foci in Experimental Model of Colitis " ;
